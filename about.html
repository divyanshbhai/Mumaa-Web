01 / Our Genesis

From Research to Global Impact.

What began as a deep-tech student project has evolved into a global benchmark for safe educational technology. MUMAA was founded on the realization that children's cognitive safety cannot be left to open-ended, unregulated AI.

By combining Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) with proprietary hardware, we’ve built an ecosystem where the AI "knows its boundaries," speaking only from verified pedagogical sources.
